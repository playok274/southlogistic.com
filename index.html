About Us

We Are Professional Logistics & cargo Agency

SOUTHERN INTERNATIONAL LOGISTICS JOINT STOCK COMPANY was founded in 2012. SOUTHLOG is member of Vietnam Logistics Association (VLA), International Federation of Freight Forwarding Association (FIATA), Worldwide 3PL Network ( W3PLN).

SOUTHLOG has a logistics team, along with a supply chain of warehousing, transportation and professional freight management systems in order to offer a full and supportive program to customer. With that, we can build a trust network of client's value and benefits.

  • Our service quality is operated worldwide based on the multitude of standardized process efforts, including sustainable Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) reviewed and growth as part of our development orientation.

  • As part of our commitment to quality criteria, we have a team of dedicated, highly qualified professionals, who consistently manage global service quality and concentrate on growing in tandem with market trends.
